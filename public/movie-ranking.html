TOP 3 ACTION THRILLER MOVIES

DRIVE 2011

A stuntman and getaway driver falls in love with Irene who is married to a criminal. In a bid to protect her from her husband and some gangsters, he decides to cross over to the other side of the law.

SICARIO 2018

To take down the drug cartels operating at the US-Mexico border, the CIA colludes with a covert operative to kidnap a kingpin's daughter. But things go horribly wrong as they try to sneak into Mexico.

SISU 2022

During the last days of World War II, a solitary prospector crosses paths with Nazis on a scorched-earth retreat in northern Finland. When the soldiers decide to steal his gold, they quickly discover they just tangled with no ordinary miner.
